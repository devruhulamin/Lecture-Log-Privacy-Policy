Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 08, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lecture Logger ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Local Storage

Lecture Logger is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

  • No User Accounts: You are not required to create an account or provide an email address to use the App.
  • Local Data: All data generated by your use of the App, including saved YouTube playlist links, custom notes, and timestamps, is stored entirely locally on your device.
  • We do not have access to your notes or study data. If you delete the app, your local data will be deleted as well.

2. Third-Party Services (YouTube API)

To function as a video player and study tool, Lecture Logger utilizes YouTube API Services to fetch and display video content.

  • By using this App, you are agreeing to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service.
  • The App uses the official YouTube player to display content and does not interact with your personal YouTube or Google account (as there is no login feature).
  • We do not access, collect, or share your YouTube watch history or API data.
  • Google may collect data via the embedded YouTube player as outlined in the Google Privacy Policy.

3. Permissions

The App requires basic internet permissions to fetch playlist metadata and stream video content directly from YouTube. We do not request access to your contacts, location, camera, or microphone.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and publishing the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
